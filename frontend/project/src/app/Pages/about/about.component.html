About Us Page

The goal of “Safety Seekers” is to create awareness among local people of Windsor city as well as among those who are visitors or people willing to migrate to Windsor regarding fire incidents that took place at different locations in Windsor city. This will help them to take necessary precautions, if they are living in or near, or visiting fire accident prone areas. In addition to it, the web app will assist emergency rescue system of the city to set up their stations/offices near such accident-prone areas in order to reduce damage to life and property.

“Safety Seekers” aims to help the local emergency rescue system and people of Windsor city by providing information regarding fire accidents and their frequency at any place in Windsor, Ontario. i.e., it will highlight the areas or localities that are prone to fire accidents on map of Windsor city, so that people who are visiting those places or are thinking to migrate are well informed about the locality and can take all the necessary precautions or look for other options. Thus, making local authorities and people aware about such locations. Furthermore, with the help of this web application, people will be able to book venue for their events (sports, exhibition, cultural, etc) and these events and their location will be displayed on map.
